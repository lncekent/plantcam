Expert Scanning Tips

Optimal Lighting

Avoid harsh shadows or direct midday sun. Soft, indirect natural light works best for capturing texture and color accuracy.

Isolate the Leaf

Focus on a single, representative leaf. Ensure the background is simple or slightly blurred to help the AI focus on the plant.

Keep it Clear

Hold your device steady. Blurry images significantly reduce diagnosis accuracy. Wipe your camera lens before scanning.
