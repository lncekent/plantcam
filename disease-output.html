Powdery Mildew confirmed with high confidence.

Symptoms

Distinct circular white, flour-like spots appearing on the upper surface of leaves.

Affected leaves may eventually turn yellow and curl upwards.

medical_services Treatment Plan

1

Prune infected leaves

Carefully remove all visible white spots. Sterilize shears between each cut to prevent further spread.

2

Use organic fungicide

Apply a mixture of potassium bicarbonate or a neem oil solution every 7 days until the issue resolves.

3

Increase air circulation

Space your plants further apart and ensure they are not touching walls to reduce local humidity.
